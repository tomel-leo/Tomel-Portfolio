WHO AM I?

About Me

Hi! I'm Olugboye Ayomide Feranmi, also known as Tomel. Just to give you a little background, I'm a Registered Nurse with a deep passion for healthcare excellence, education, and leveraging technology to improve patient outcomes.

I graduated from Olabisi Onabanjo University (BNSc), where I was actively involved in student leadership and initiatives that support learning and personal development. I enjoy reducing complex healthcare challenges to simple, effective, and patient-centered solutions.

When I'm not providing patient care or developing healthcare projects, you can find me mentoring nursing students, exploring digital health tools, or working on community health initiatives. I believe in creating solutions that bring people together and generate positive impact.

My mission is to bridge the gap between traditional nursing practice and modern technology, making healthcare more accessible, efficient, and patient-friendly.
